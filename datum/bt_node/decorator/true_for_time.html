true_for_time
/datum/bt_node/decorator/true_for_time

Passes while its duration has not elapsed. Starts timing on the first tick; resets when the tree resets.

duration: how long to pass, in deciseconds (e.g. "10 SECONDS"). succeed_on_timeout FALSE (default): counts as succeed and lets lower nodes pass
